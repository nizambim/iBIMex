BIM Services

Our comprehensive BIM services ensure that all project information requirements are met with precision and efficiency.

3D BIM Modeling

Detailed 3D modeling services that provide accurate spatial representation of all building components and systems.

  • Architectural modeling
  • Structural modeling
  • MFP systems modeling

4D Scheduling

Integration of project schedules with 3D models to visualize construction sequences and optimize project timelines.

  • Construction sequencing
  • Timeline visualization
  • Resource optimization

5D Cost Estimation

Linking cost data to BIM elements for accurate quantity takeoffs and budget forecasting throughout the project lifecycle.

  • Quantity takeoffs
  • Cost analysis
  • Budget forecasting

Clash Detection

Advanced interference checking to identify and resolve spatial conflicts before they impact construction.

  • Interference checking
  • Conflict resolution
  • Coordination reporting

BIM Coordination

Comprehensive coordination services to ensure all disciplines work together seamlessly within the BIM environment.

  • Multidisciplinary coordination
  • Coordination meetings
  • Issue tracking and resolution

6D Facility Management

Integration of asset data for efficient facility management and maintenance throughout the building lifecycle.

  • Asset management
  • Maintenance scheduling
  • Lifecycle management
